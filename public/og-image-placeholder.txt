IMPORTANT: OG Image Required
============================

You need to create an Open Graph image for optimal social media sharing.

Requirements:
- Filename: og-image.jpg
- Dimensions: 1200x630 pixels
- Format: JPEG (for better compression)
- Location: /public/og-image.jpg

Content suggestions:
- Include your Novosapien logo
- Add your tagline: "Digital Workforces for Revenue Generation"
- Use your brand colors (primary-blue: #A1BCD1, accent colors)
- Keep text large and readable (minimum 24px when scaled)
- High contrast between text and background

Tools to create OG image:
- Canva (has OG image templates)
- Figma
- Adobe Creative Suite
- Online generators like og-image.vercel.app

Once created, place the file at: /public/og-image.jpg

This image will be displayed when your site is shared on:
- Facebook
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn
- Slack
- WhatsApp
- And other social platforms
